## Description

LexFlow is the most advanced AI-powered legal practice management platform. It is designed for law firms, solo attorneys, legal departments, and legal professionals worldwide. The platform offers comprehensive case management, unique document version control with AI-powered comparison, client CRM, calendar scheduling, analytics, and team collaboration features.

## Key Differentiators

1. AI-Powered Document Analysis and Comparison
2. Full Document Version Control (like Git for legal documents)
3. Multi-language support (English, Russian, Tajik)
4. Affordable pricing starting at $7/month
5. Modern tech stack (React, Go, PostgreSQL)

## Features List

- AI Legal Assistant (OpenAI API, GPT-5 mini/GPT-5.2)
- Case Management with Timeline and Tasks
- Document Version Control and Comparison
- Client CRM (Individuals and Legal Entities)
- Calendar and Meetings Management
- Analytics and Reports Dashboard
- Team Collaboration (Role-based Access)
